Description

This track represents the pseudo-homologous regions (PHRs) identified with the draft human pangenome from the Human Pangenome Reference Consortium. The PHRs are regions indicative of recombination between heterologous (non-homologous) human acrocentric chromosomes. For each PHR, the average Shannon Diversity Index is reported (see the Methods section below).

Methods

  • We applied PGGB to construct a pangenome variation graph from centromere-spanning acrocentric contigs of the human pangenome.
  • We untangled the pangenome variation graph by applying ODGI UNTANGLE.
  • To quantify the degree of disorder in the untangling result, we calculated the Shannon diversity index (SDI) in windows 50 kb long across the different acrocentrics.
  • We merged intervals that were less than 30 kbps apart and then removed intervals shorter than 30 kbps.
For a full or a description of the methods used, refer to Guarracino et al. (2023) in the References section below.
